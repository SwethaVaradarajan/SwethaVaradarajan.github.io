Bio

Swetha Varadarajan was a graphics hardware engineer specialized in machine learning (ML) and high performance computing (HPC) performance projection and validation. Her experience in HPC involved developing GPU and CPU runtime libraries, optimizing multi-process communication and accelerating applications.
